Dairy-Free Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Description

A good chocolate chip cookie recipe for lactose-intolerant folks, made with gluten-free flour, vegan butter, and vegan chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter, softended
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups vegan chocolate chips

Steps

  1. 1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F / 165 degrees C.
  2. 2. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  3. 3. Whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  4. 4. Beat brown sugar and vegan butter in a separate large bowl for 2-3 minutes.
  5. 5. Mix eggs and vanilla in with brown sugar and vegan butter.
  6. 6. Add flour, baking soda, and salt mixture to the large bowl mixture and mix until combined.
  7. 7. Fold in vegan chocolate chips to the large bowl mixture. Once folded in, you have cookie dough! :)
  8. 8. Drop small spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto the prepared baking sheets.
  9. 9. Put baking sheets in the preheated oven and bake until edges are golden, rotating halfway through about 10 minutes.
  10. 10. Take baking sheets out of oven, let cookies cool on the baking sheet briefly before moving them to a wire rack to cool completely.
