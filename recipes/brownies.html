Dairy-Free Brownies Recipe

Home

Description

A good brownie recipe for lactose-intolerant folks. Feel free to tweak to your liking.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vegan semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 teaspoons instant coffee granules
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/3 cup gluten-free flour
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon coffee-flavored liqueur
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F / 175 degrees C.
  2. Put chocolate chips and canola oil in a large glass bowl together. Heat in a microwave for 30 seconds, then take out and stir. Repeat 4-6 times or until completely melted.
  3. Stir eggs and coffee granules into the melted chocolate.
  4. In a separate bowl, stir sugar, cocoa powder, flour, and baking soda together. Once sifted, pour this into the chocolate bowl and stir to incorporate fully.
  5. Pour coffee liqueur and vanilla extract into the chocolate bowl and stir. Once stirred, you have brownie batter! :)
  6. Pour brownie batter into an 8-inch square baking dish.
  7. Put dish in preheated oven and bake until you start to see drying along the edges. This usually takes 20-25 minutes, but keep an eye on it as time may vary.
